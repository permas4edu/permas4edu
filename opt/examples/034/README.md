$$ m = \frac{1}{4} \rho \pi h \left( d_o^2-d_i^2 \right) $$ 

$$ J_p = \frac{m}{8} \left( d_o^2 + d_i^2 \right) \Rightarrow  d_o = \sqrt{\frac{8 J_p}{m} -d_i^2} $$

$$ J_d = \frac{1}{2} J_p + \frac{1}{12} m h^2 \Rightarrow h = \sqrt{\frac{12(J_d-0.5 J_p)}{m}} $$
